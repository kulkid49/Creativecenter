Targeted Support for Complex Challenges

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy helps you explore patterns, emotions, and beliefs that shape your life. You'll develop self-awareness, healthier coping strategies, and tools for long-term resilience.

Couples & Family Therapy

Whether addressing conflict or rebuilding trust, this therapy encourages open dialogue, empathy, and shared understanding—restoring balance within relationships.

Group Therapy

Group therapy offers a sense of belonging and shared growth with mutual support and skills that can be integrated into daily life for lasting change.

Expertise You Can Trust

SCHWARTZ-MERCER

OWNER

Schwartz-Mercer is the Owner and Clinical Director of the Center for Creative Living. She brings over three decades of experience in mental health, couples work, grief counseling, and integrative psychotherapy. With more than 30 years of experience, her specialties include trauma therapy, depression, anxiety, grief counseling, support for chronic and terminal illness, death and dying, adoption issues, and divorce stress.

  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW)
  • Certified in Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)
  • Member, National Association of Social Workers (NASW)
  • Psychoanalytically trained
